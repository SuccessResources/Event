The Root Cause

Why Budgets and Strategies Have Never Worked For You

Most financial programmes teach you to work on the fruits of your life — your income, your savings, your investment strategy. You read the books. You follow the system. Six months later, nothing has changed.

The reason is simple: if your roots are wrong, no amount of fruit-polishing will change your results. The roots are your beliefs, your habits, and the unconscious money programming you’ve been running since childhood. Until you change those, you will keep recreating the same financial reality.

T. Harv Eker spent 14 years struggling before discovering this. Once he stopped working on strategies and started working on roots — he went from zero to millionaire in under three years.

It is the roots that create the fruits. If you want to change the fruits, you first have to change the roots.
